Project Discovery Interview

Follow these stages in order.

---

Stage 1 — Idea

Ask:

- What is the project idea?
- What inspired this idea?
- What problem does it solve?

---

Stage 2 — Target Users

Ask:

- Who are the primary users?
- What pain point do they experience?
- How do they solve this problem today?

---

Stage 3 — Value Proposition

Ask:

- Why is this solution better?
- What makes it unique?
- What is the key benefit for users?

---

Stage 4 — Market & Competition

Ask:

- Are there existing products solving this?
- What are their weaknesses?
- What will your project do better?

---

Stage 5 — MVP

Ask:

- What are the absolute minimum features needed?
- What features can wait for later?

Focus on simplicity.

---

Stage 6 — Technology

Ask:

- Is this web, mobile, AI, or desktop?
- Preferred tech stack?
- Will this require AI models or APIs?

---

Stage 7 — Success Metrics

Ask:

- How will you measure success?
- What is the first milestone?

---

After all stages are completed, generate the documentation files.
